Cybersecurity Tips

Simple habits that make a big difference in staying safe online.

Everyday Tips

  • Don't use the same password. If someone gets the password they could have access to everything.
  • Don't leave your devices unlocked and alone in public.
  • Make sure to have every device and account you own have a unique password, this makes sure that if someone guesses one of your passwords they won't be able to use the other devices.
  • Think before clicking unfamiliar links
  • Use privacy settings on social media
  • Only use trusted Wi-Fi networks. Free Public Wifi like at McDonalds isn't very secure and people on that network can harm your devices connected to it.
  • Backup important files regularly to make sure no lose of important files happen if a virus gets on your computer.
  • Make sure to use your Antivirus to scan your computer. You can automate it by scheduling scans daily, weekly, or monthly
