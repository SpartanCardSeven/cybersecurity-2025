Security Practices

Understanding proper security behavior can reduce cyber risk significantly.

Best Practices

  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Keep systems updated
  • Be cautious with email attachments

Tools To Use

  • Password managers: These can help you keep all your passwords in one place and it's encyrpted to keep it safe.
  • 2 Step Verification: Using this on any service that provides this makes sure that it's you and not someone who shouldn't that has access to your account info.
  • Antivirus: This tool can be very helpful to keep your devices safe. Downloading stuff can allow the person to send a virus or malware to harm your device, Antivirus helps scan and get rid of any harmful files.
  • VPN: VPN's encrypt your internet connection making it more safe to do online browsing and shopping
